Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

At GOLAOWN, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely happy with your items, we offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery.

Conditions for Return

• Items must be in their original condition (unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached).

• Items must be returned in their original packaging.

• Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) is required.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest turnaround time and item availability.

How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact our support team at support@golaown.com with your order number and reason for return.

2. Once approved, you will receive a Return Authorization and instructions on where to send your package.

3. Pack your items securely and ship them back to us using a trackable shipping method.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.
